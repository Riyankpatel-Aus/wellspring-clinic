What is BMI?

Body Mass Index (BMI) is a measure of body fat based on height and weight that applies to adult men and women. It's a useful tool to screen for weight categories that may lead to health problems.

BMI Categories

Underweight: BMI less than 18.5
Normal: BMI 18.5 - 24.9
Overweight: BMI 25 - 29.9
Obese: BMI 30 or greater
